Details

  • Tap badge → expands, collapses on second tap (or ✕ button)
  • Only one badge expanded at a time — tapping a second collapses the first
  • Chip overflow: "+N more" chip shown when list is truncated (chips wrap up to ~2 rows)
  • Aggregate achievements (totalVisited, totalPercent, achievementsUnlocked, gameCompleted): show progress + context hint, no chip list
  • Region names: resolved from feature.properties.name in each tracker's GeoJSON
