BUG 1 Bottom sheet cut off at top on mobile

When dragging the sheet to full height, content at the top is hidden under the status bar / safe area.

Fix: Add padding-top: env(safe-area-inset-top, 44px) to the sheet's full-height snap point, and ensure max-height accounts for the safe area.
BUG 2 No settings menu

Dark/light toggle is buried in the sidebar header. No central place for preferences.

Fix: Add a Settings section at the bottom of ProfileScreen (mobile) and the sidebar (desktop). Includes: dark/light toggle, reset options, onboarding re-trigger, app version. Removes the theme toggle from the header.
BUG 3 Redundant Stats button in map header

The 📊 stats button appears in the sidebar header on mobile, but Stats is already accessible from the Profile tab.

Fix: Remove the stats icon button from Sidebar.jsx and WorldSidebar.jsx headers on mobile. Keep it on desktop.
BUG 4 Redundant Friends button in map header

The 👥 friends icon appears in the sidebar header on mobile, but Friends is already the Social tab.

Fix: Remove friends icon button from headers on mobile. Keep on desktop sidebar.
BUG 5 Floating games + layer buttons mispositioned

The 🎮 games button and 🗺 map layer button float in the middle of the map instead of anchored top-right.

Fix: Anchor both to top-right corner of the map container with consistent right: 12px and stacked top values. These only show on desktop — on mobile, Games is accessible from the Explore tab.
