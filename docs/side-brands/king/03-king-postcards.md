# King Plan 03 — King Postcards

**Goal:** Create a best-in-class social output layer for travel progress.

## Feature Ideas

- Luxury share-card themes with cleaner typography and stronger map framing
- Auto-generated “trip chapters” from dates, notes, and photos
- End-of-month / end-of-trip postcard exports
- Public mini-pages for selected travel stories

## MVP

- 3 premium postcard templates
- Export for portrait + square layouts
- Optional profile CTA / watermark
- Stronger empty states so even light users get a polished result

## Why It Matters

This is one of the easiest ways to make the product feel expensive, social, and differentiated without needing huge backend changes first.
