# King Plan 05 — King Clubs

**Goal:** Build prestige-oriented social structures beyond 1:1 friendships.

## Concepts

- Invite-only clubs around themes (Europe speedrun, UNESCO collectors, national parks, islands)
- Club leaderboards
- Shared challenge boards
- Seasonal club competitions
- Club showcase pages for public profiles

## MVP

- Private clubs with 5-20 members
- Club feed with milestone posts
- Monthly leaderboard reset option
- Club-only badges

## Dependencies

- Public profiles / social feed foundation
- Moderation basics
- Premium / plan logic if clubs are monetized
